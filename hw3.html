Another Javascript Calculator!

Herein lies yet another rendition of a Javascript Calulator. Essentially, it prints out each year's return for a given investment at a given annual percent return. Interest is compounded annually.

Instructions:

Enten whole numbers for "Principle" investment and "Interest Rate". For example, if you want to calculate ten years of return data for an initial investment of $10,000.00 at an interest rate of 8% (or 0.08), simply enter 10000 and 8. Values other than whole numbers will cause this script to error out.
